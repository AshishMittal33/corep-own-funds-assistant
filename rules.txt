# PRA COREP REGULATORY RULES
# Based on CRR and EBA Implementing Technical Standards

## COREP TEMPLATE C 01.00 - OWN FUNDS

### CET1 CAPITAL (Common Equity Tier 1)
**CRR Article 26 - Components of CET1:**
1. Ordinary share capital (common stock) issued by the institution
2. Share premium accounts related to CET1 instruments
3. Retained earnings
4. Accumulated other comprehensive income
5. Other reserves

**COREP Reporting Codes:**
- Row 010: Ordinary share capital
- Row 020: Share premium account  
- Row 030: Retained earnings
- Row 040: Other comprehensive income
- Row 050: Other reserves

### DEDUCTIONS FROM CET1
**CRR Article 36 - Mandatory Deductions:**
1. Intangible assets (goodwill, software, brands) - deduct from CET1
2. Deferred tax assets that rely on future profitability
3. Defined benefit pension fund deficits

**COREP Reporting Codes:**
- Row 070: Intangible assets (deduction)
- Row 080: Deferred tax assets (deduction)
- Row 090: Pension fund deficits (deduction)

### CALCULATION FORMULAS
**Total CET1 (Row 100) =**
(010 + 020 + 030 + 040 + 050) - (070 + 080 + 090)

### VALIDATION RULES
1. CET1 must be ≥ 4.5% of Risk Weighted Assets (RWA)
2. Total deductions ≤ 15% of gross CET1 components
3. All amounts must be positive except deductions
4. Intangible assets must be fully deducted

### EXAMPLE:
Bank has:
- Ordinary shares: £100,000,000 (Row 010)
- Share premium: £50,000,000 (Row 020)
- Retained earnings: £200,000,000 (Row 030)
- Intangible assets: £30,000,000 (Row 070)

CET1 = £100M + £50M + £200M - £30M = £320,000,000 (Row 100)
